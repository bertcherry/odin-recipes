Scones

Description

This recipe is a favorite, because it's adaptable to any ingredients in your cupboard. Try out the classic cherry almond variety, or mix it up with fluffy pumpkin ginger scones!

Ingredients

  • 8T butter
  • 1/4c sugar
  • 2c flour
  • 1T baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2c cream/milk/yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp almond extract
  • 3/4c dried cherries

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cut together butter and sugar.
  3. Mix in flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat the egg with almond extract and cream/milk/yogurt of your choosing, then add to flour mixture.
  5. Fold in cherries.
  6. Bake in a round cake or pie pan for 35-45 minutes until cooked through.
